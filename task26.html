Document Digitization

Currently organizations track policies and other organizations documents in word/ pdf. Whereas a digitized document can provide a comprehensive tracking of recent modifications, including ownership, authorship, version logs thereby facilitating more effective monitoring and management. Overall this is the ask from the regulators to maintain all the documents digitally.
